Welcome To Integrated Development Network Kenya

Integrated Development Network (IDN) is an international NGO formed in 2016 and registered by the NGOs Co-ordination Board in the Republic of Kenya. The organization was formed through joint indigenous intellectual efforts with the help of the local appealing force on the ground.

The formation of IDN was prompted by sad effects of the disputed 2007 general election and its subsequent violent post – election crisis that resulted in social-economic setbacks. To these effects, a lot of unnecessary killings, intimidations, humiliation,looting, raping and gross human right violation aggravated the social stability in the country. Though Kenya was able to secure a new constitution in 2010, and though the 2013 general election was by and large peaceful it was also very tense particularly due to the petition lodged by the CORD coalition against the results of the presidential elections. The year 2016 already witnessed violent protests around the question of the IEBC.

It is within this background that the IDN was formed with the express aim of advocating, lobbying, providing information, education, communication, providing capacity and technical assistance in promoting peace, security, and national cohesion in Kenya. In 2016 and 2017 IDN shall focus on the 2017 general election.
