STAKEHOLDERS FORUM MEETING ON PEACE, SECURITY & NATIONAL COHESION

The Event was Organized by Integrated Development Network(IDN), Stakeholders drawn across the Board( IEBC, Registrar of Political Parties, NCIC, National Police Service, NIS, Foreign Missions, Business Fraternity, Government representatives, SUPKEM, KEPSA, KNCCI, COTU, Students Leaders, MPs, MCA's among others converged at Hotel Intercontinental for a round table discussion on matters touching on Security and Cohesion ahead of the 8th August,2017 General Elections.

Election Preparedness, Role of the Carter Center in Election Observation and Monitoring ahead of the August 2017.

Mr. Fergus Anderson;Head of Security Election Observation Mission Kenya: 2017 from The CARTER CENTER FOUNDATION(USA) paid a courtesy call to IDN-KENYA. Issues discussed; Election Preparedness, Role of the Carter Center in Election Observation and Monitoring ahead of the August 2017. Issues shared also : IDN Kenya Vision ,Mission,Objectives and Calendar of events as we approach the General Elections.The bottom line statement was need for Peace and Cohesion as a Country.

PEACE TALENT SEARCH

Nairobi belongs to All of us irrespective of our diversity in tribe, religion, race or color. Nairobi remains the Most Populous City in Kenya with all sub-tribes in place. Its our Socio-Economic and Political bedrock. When "Nairobi'' speaks, the rest of Kenyans follow-suit ; When "Nairobi" says Peace, Majority of Kenyans follow-suit. IDN Kenya together with Tecla Lorupe Foundation, The Mzazi Foundation and WestWood Entertainment Family ; United in solidarity for the Peace Talent Search through Sports and entertainment, that saw Politicians across the Nairobi County converge at Bora Bora Restaraunt Dagorretti North and vow to uphold and preach messages of Peace and Cohesion and accept Election results Come 8th August, 2017.
