ABOUT US

Our Vision

A developed and socially more responsive community at peace with itself and its neighbours.

Our Mission

To preserve, strengthen, and promote voluntary action in pursuit of a more just and equitable society.

Core Values

IDN is guided in delivering its services by the following core values: -

  • - Sincerity and honesty
  • - Equity and fairness
  • - Accountability and transparency
  • - Commitment and cooperation
  • - Professionalism and voluntary service
  • - Gender sensitivity
  • - Operates as not for profit and non-political
Our Objectives
  • • To promote peace-building processes.
  • • To support the field presence and activities by fostering cooperation with key stakeholders, partners, non-governmental organisations working in the field of peace-building in order to promote good values.
  • • To develop the competences peace ambassadors in human rights promotion and protection, conflict transformation, peace-building and intercultural dialogue, as well as other specific competences according to the participants’ needs, related to their role as future ambassadors.
  • • To support and give visibility to projects for peace-building in conflict-affected areas and communities.
  • • To enhance the youth ambassadors capacity to take action at a local level in environments affected by previous or ongoing conflicts.
  • • To institutionalize good governance in public systems by building national capacity and leadership.
  • • To raise awareness on the impact of corruption in the public sector and bring this to the national policy agenda.
  • • To increase transparency and accountability in public regulatory and management systems.
  • • To promote individual and institutional integrity in the public sector.
  • • To create and support a peace ambassadors to secure the sustainability.
